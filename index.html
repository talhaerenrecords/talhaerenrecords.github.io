ABOUT US

We have put a lot of effort into reaching the days where we can prepare this text here. Many of you know us from Spotify and the dark side of the music industry. But who are we actually, and how was Talha Eren Records created?

Initially, through the introduction of a few mutual friends, we came together and made our name known by sharing Phonk songs, surpassing nearly 5 million monthly listeners and around 150 million streams in total. Talha Eren Records was founded in February 2022 by individuals among us, including names like Enes Emre, Shizou, Estawky, Efkan (also known as Syrus Inc) and Fatih.

In a short period of time, as the rapidly rising Talha Eren Records team, we have established ourselves as a label for our audience. We take pride in having talented individuals involved in the realm of Phonk music within our organization..
